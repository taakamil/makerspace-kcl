Welcome to the maKe Makerspace

We're excited to welcome you to the maKe Makerspace - King's College London's premier collaborative workspace for innovation and creativity.

What You'll Find Here

Our makerspace is equipped with state-of-the-art tools and technology to support your creative and research endeavors:

  • 3D Printing Facilities: Multiple printers for rapid prototyping and modeling
  • Electronics Workstations: Complete setups for circuit design and testing
  • Programming Resources: Development environments and microcontroller kits
  • Collaboration Spaces: Areas designed for team projects and brainstorming

Getting Started

Before you begin working in the makerspace, we recommend:

  1. Review our training modules for safety and equipment orientation
  2. Explore our current projects for inspiration
  3. Contact us at make@kcl.ac.uk to schedule an orientation session
